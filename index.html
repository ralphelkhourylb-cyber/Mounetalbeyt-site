Our Story

Our story begins in 1960, with "Samir Rachid El Khoury", the original founder. With a deep love for Lebanese traditions and a passion for authentic flavors, Samir started a small local business rooted in the art of "mouneh"—preserving seasonal produce using time-honored methods passed down through generations. What began as a modest operation quickly gained a reputation for quality and taste, with Samir’s pickles and olives becoming a staple in homes and restaurants across the region.

Today, the legacy lives on through his son, "Elie Samir El Khoury", who proudly carries the family tradition forward. Under Elie’s leadership, the business continues to thrive, offering premium Lebanese "mouneh", pickles, and olives to restaurants and local customers alike. Staying true to the original values of quality, authenticity, and fair pricing, the business remains a trusted name for those seeking the best that Lebanese cuisine has to offer.
